About Me

A product builder at heart

I'm a product leader with over 15 years of experience building and scaling financial products that matter. Based in New York City, I've worked across payments, lending, wealth management, and embedded finance — always with a focus on creating meaningful experiences that drive real business outcomes.

My superpower is bridging the gap between deep technical complexity and human-centered design. I believe the best financial products are the ones customers don't have to think about — they just work, seamlessly and reliably.

When I'm not thinking about product strategy or roadmaps, you'll find me painting, exploring NYC's art scene, or experimenting in the kitchen.
