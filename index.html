About Me

A product builder at heart

I'm a Fintech product leader with 15+ years of experience building Fintech products and platforms across multiple US and global financial institutions. I've worked across payments, credit cards and wealth management ecosystems — always with a focus on driving transparency and great experience within the financial ecosystem that also drives business outcomes.

I also serve as the NY Chapter Head for Products that Count, focusing on fostering a product community in the NY Area. When I'm not thinking about product strategy or roadmaps, you can find me painting, tinkering with AI coding tools, or spending time playing with my 6-year-old.
